A quick Introduction

I started drawing and doodling earlier at school, and at the age of 16, I learnt techniques like water and oil painting. Bob Ross inspired me to take up "Wet on Wet" oil painting techniques and reproduced several paintings. The doodle designed for Google made an appereance in the 'Chip' magazine in 2010. After drawing a whole lot of inspiration from the comics, I started pursuing robotics. I built robots and retrofitted machines with automation systems built from own designed circuit boards and additional machine design. Quite soon after I developed inhouse IoT framework with quintessential functions to monitor these machines built. Artificial Intelligence has come across my mind lately leading me to pursue a masters degree in Autonomous Systems Engineering in Germany.
